Shanghai Museum

 

 

Description

The Shanghai Museum is one of the leading museums in China, located in the city of Shanghai. It houses a vast collection of historical and cultural artifacts from China and around the world.

History

The museum was founded in 1952 and has since grown in size and importance. Today, it is a renowned international institution, attracting millions of visitors every year.
