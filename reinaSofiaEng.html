Reina Sofia Museum

 

 

Description

The Museo Nacional Centro de Arte Reina Sofia, or simply Reina Sofia Museum, is the Spanish national museum of 20th-century art, located in Madrid. It holds one of the largest and most significant collections of modern and contemporary art in the world.

History

The Reina Sofia Museum was officially opened in 1992 and is named after Queen Sofia of Spain. It is housed in the former San Carlos Hospital, an 18th-century neoclassical building. The museum is renowned for housing the famous painting "Guernica" by Pablo Picasso.
