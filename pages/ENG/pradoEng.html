Prado Museum

 

 

Description

The Prado Museum, located in Madrid, Spain, is one of the most important art museums in the world. It is especially famous for its collection of Spanish paintings.

History

The Prado Museum was inaugurated in 1819 as a natural history museum, but it was soon converted into an art museum. It houses works by some of the greatest Spanish artists, such as Velázquez, Goya, and El Greco.
