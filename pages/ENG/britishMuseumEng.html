British Museum

 

 

Description

The British Museum is dedicated to the history and culture of humanity, with a vast collection of artifacts from around the world.

History

The British Museum was founded in 1753, making it the world's first public national museum. It currently has one of the largest and most comprehensive collections of artifacts in the world, with more than 8 million works in its possession.
