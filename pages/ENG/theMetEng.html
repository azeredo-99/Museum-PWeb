The Met - Metropolitan Museum of Art

 

 

Description

The Metropolitan Museum of Art, popularly known as The Met, is one of the largest and most important art museums in the world. Located in New York City, the museum houses a vast permanent collection that includes artworks from various cultures and historical periods.

History

The Met was founded in 1870 and officially opened its doors on February 20, 1872. Since then, the museum has expanded its collection and facilities, becoming a renowned international cultural institution.
